Content Notes

Target personas: New IAM Developers,Developers new to Okta

Description: Overview of JSON web tokens.

Why is this here?: - External Oktane 2023 - MatchIt: 21% of respondents got JWTs incorrect in the MatchIt exercise.

  • External Oktane 2023 - GroupIt:
    • 9.3% were unable to categorize JSON web token
    • Respondents placed JSON web tokens under the broad APIS and SDKs group at 48.8% which was somewhat of a “catch-all” group. Placing topics under this group may indicate lack of confidence of what the term meant.

Structure type: Concept

Content type: Conceptual overview

Conceptual overviews present a high-level introduction to a topic. For example, there are product \ feature overviews:

  • https://developer.okta.com/docs/concepts/multi-tenancy/
  • https://developer.okta.com/docs/concepts/scim/
  • https://developer.okta.com/docs/concepts/auth-servers/

And there are also overviews of lower-level material. For example:

  • The Client Credentials Flow: Taking this material from the top to “Set Up Your App”. https://developer.okta.com/docs/guides/implement-grant-type/clientcreds/main/
  • Access, ID, and Refresh Tokens: Based on the non-curl material from this document and new content to explain what they are and why they are necessary. https://developer.okta.com/docs/guides/refresh-tokens/main/
  • https://developer.okta.com/docs/concepts/okta-data-model/

Such high-level overviews provide developers with a broad understanding of the system’s workings. Deeper discussions into single topics are called Explanations.
